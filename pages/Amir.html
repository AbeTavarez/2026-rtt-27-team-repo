Why GLEAN should be in every school!

GLEAN is a powerful tool that can greatly enhance the learning experience in schools. It provides students with access to a wealth of information and resources, allowing them to explore Global topics in depth and at their own pace. With GLEAN, students can develop critical thinking skills, improve their research abilities, and get to know students from all over the world! GLEAN can help teachers create more engaging and interactive lessons, making learning more enjoyable for students. Overall, incorporating GLEAN into every school can lead to improved academic performance and a more enriching educational experience for all students.
